# DESIGN.md

## Overview
Beyond Label Gifts uses a high-contrast luxury gift aesthetic built around midnight surfaces, champagne gold typography, radiant lavender accents, and layered glow effects. The site should feel intimate, elevated, and emotionally persuasive on both mobile and desktop.

## Design Tokens

### Colors
- `--background`: deep near-black with plum undertones
- `--foreground`: warm ivory for body text
- `--primary`: champagne gold
- `--accent`: lavender glow
- `--muted`: charcoal plum surfaces
- `--border`: subtle luminous edge

### Typography
- Display: elegant serif for brand headlines and luxury product framing
- Body: clear modern sans-serif for product clarity and conversion copy
- Script accent: sparingly used for emotional emphasis and signature moments

## Layout
- Long-scroll storytelling on the homepage with a dominant hero product stage
- Strong asymmetry, large spacing swings, and image-first sections
- Compact, high-trust sticky actions on mobile

## Components
- Fixed tubelight navigation shared across routes
- Interactive product stage with finish toggle and parallax rotation
- Feature strips, editorial article rows, and story-led content sections
- Premium footer with brand promise and legal navigation

## Motion
- Slow floating product motion, scroll cues, reveal choreography, and glowing SVG atmospherics
- Reduced-motion fallback via CSS and restrained transitions
